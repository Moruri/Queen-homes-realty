Description

Welcome to Amethyst, a luxury apartment development located in the heart of Kilimani, one of Nairobi's most sought-after neighborhoods. This stunning 3-bedroom apartment offers contemporary living with premium finishes throughout.

The spacious open-plan living and dining area features large windows that flood the space with natural light. The modern kitchen comes fully fitted with quality cabinets, granite countertops, and ample storage. Each bedroom is generously sized with built-in wardrobes, and the master bedroom includes an en-suite bathroom.

Residents enjoy access to a rooftop terrace, gym, children's play area, and 24/7 security. The location offers easy access to schools, shopping centers, hospitals, and major transport routes. This is an excellent opportunity for homeowners and investors alike.
