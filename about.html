Home About Us — Real Estate Agency in Kenya

Queen Homes Realty is a prominent player in the real estate industry, known for its innovation, integrity, and commitment to excellence. Founded in 2023, Queen Homes Realty has rapidly grown into a trusted name in residential real estate markets across Nairobi and beyond.

Our Mission

To connect clients with quality, well-curated homes and investment opportunities through professionalism, integrity, and personalized service. We aim to simplify the real estate journey while delivering value, comfort, and long-term wealth for every client.

Our Vision

To become Kenya's most trusted and admired real estate brand — a one-stop shop for property solutions serving investors across the globe, delivering exceptional properties and creating lifestyles fit for royalty.
